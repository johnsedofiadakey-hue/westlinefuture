The 8 Project Stages
What happens, when, and who does it
1
📋  Onboarding, Payment & Site Survey
Your project begins — we meet your space, you meet your portal
Client + Westline ~10 days
Westline does
  • Creates your project account and client portal
  • Assigns your dedicated project manager
  • Schedules and dispatches the technical survey team
  • Takes precise measurements, photos, and structural notes
  • Records electrical points, plumbing, and any site constraints
You need to
  • Log in to your portal using the link sent by your PM
  • Pay the rendering fee in the Financials tab
  • Confirm your site survey date and time with your PM
  • Ensure the full space is accessible on the survey day
  • Share existing floor plans if you have them (optional)
📋  Rendering fee invoice — Financials tab
No other documents required from you
2
🎨  3D Rendering Review
See your space before anything is built — review, revise, and approve
Client action ~10 days
Westline does
  • Builds detailed 3D model from verified site measurements
  • Applies finishes, materials, and colours per your project brief
  • Uploads the rendering to your Designs tab and notifies you
  • Revises based on your feedback until you are fully satisfied
You need to
  • Open the Designs tab in your portal when notified
  • Review layout, dimensions, materials, and colours carefully
  • Click Request Changes if anything needs adjusting — be specific
  • Click Approve Design when you are satisfied
🎨  3D Rendering — Designs tab (uploaded by Westline)
3
💳  Commercial Approval & Production Authority
Four sequential steps — agree cost, sign contract, pay deposit, authorise production
Client action ~14 days 60% payment
Westline does
  • Prepares versioned quotation from approved design
  • Issues quotation to your portal for review
  • Prepares contract once quotation is agreed
  • Prepares Final Scope & Deliverables document after payment
You need to — in this order
  • Step 1: Review and approve the Quotation (or request changes / counter offer) in the Documents tab
  • Step 2: Read and electronically sign the Contract via SMS code
  • Step 3: Pay the 60% Initial Deposit in the Financials tab
  • Step 4: Authorise the Final Scope & Deliverables document
📄  Quotation
📄  Signed Contract
📄  Final Scope & Deliverables
All available in Documents tab
💰  60% Initial Deposit due at this stage
Paid via Financials tab (mobile money or card) before production begins
4
🏭  Procurement & Production
Your furniture is being custom-built at our manufacturing facility
Westline working ~30 days
Westline does
  • Sources and procures all materials, hardware, and finishes
  • Custom cuts, processes, and fabricates every component
  • Runs full quality control inspection against your specification
  • Packs components securely for international shipment
You need to
  • Nothing is required from you at this stage
  • Your portal shows production is active
  • Your PM may share progress photos via WhatsApp
  • Use the Messages tab for any questions
No documents required from you at this stage
5
🚛  Shipping & Delivery
Your goods are on their way — track the shipment and settle balances on arrival
Westline manages ~35 days 30% + 10% payment
Westline does
  • Arranges international freight from production facility
  • Publishes vessel name, container number, and ETA to your portal
  • Manages customs clearance and port handling (included in price)
  • Confirms goods arrival in Ghana
  • Activates 30% and 10% balance invoices in your portal
  • Prepares installation invoice if you have requested it
You need to
  • Track your shipment live in the Overview tab (vessel + ETA shown)
  • Pay the 30% Production Balance when goods arrive in Ghana
  • Pay the 10% Final Arrival Balance at the same time
  • Pay the Installation Invoice if you have requested installation
🚢  Shipping Manifest — Documents tab (uploaded by Westline)
No documents required from you
💰  30% + 10% balances due when goods arrive in Ghana
Both invoices appear in your Financials tab simultaneously — pay via mobile money or card
6
🔧  Installation
Our technical crew fits and finishes every piece on-site — full-service projects only
Crew on-site ~7 days
Westline does
  • Assigns and dispatches the installation crew to your site
  • Prepares and secures the site before work begins
  • Assembles and fits all components per the approved 3D design
  • Installs all hardware, finishes surfaces, aligns every component
  • Uploads daily progress photos to your portal Documents tab
  • Runs an internal completion check before handover inspection
You need to
  • Keep the site clear and accessible on each scheduled day
  • Do not move or alter work in progress between sessions
  • Raise any concerns directly with the crew lead while on-site — much easier to resolve during installation than after
  • Check daily photos in your Documents tab
📸  Daily installation photos — Documents tab (uploaded by crew)
Site access / gate pass if required by your estate
7
🔎  Inspection & Sign-off
Walk through the completed work and formally accept it via the portal checklist
Client + Westline ~3 days
Westline does
  • Notifies you that installation is complete and ready for inspection
  • Your PM may walk through the space with you in person
  • Resolves any minor snags raised before sign-off is requested
You need to
  • Open the Approvals tab in your portal
  • Tick all 7 items on the inspection checklist (see below)
  • Add optional notes for any observations
  • Click "Sign Off — I Accept the Completed Work"
  • Do not sign off until any issues are fully resolved
Inspection Checklist — all 7 items must be ticked before sign-off
Workmanship quality meets expectations
All items match the agreed scope and specifications
All surfaces are clean, finished, and undamaged
Hardware, fittings, and mechanisms are functional
Dimensions and placement are as agreed
No visible defects, scratches, or damage observed
Site is clear and free of installation debris
8
🌟  Handover & Closeout
Your project is complete — collect your certificate and share your experience
Project complete ~1 day
Westline does
  • Issues your Handover Certificate to the Documents tab
  • Activates your warranty per the terms in your contract
  • Formally closes your project file
You need to
  • Open the Approvals tab in your portal
  • Rate your overall experience (1–5 stars)
  • Write a short review of your project journey
  • Optionally consent to us sharing your review as a testimonial
  • Click "Submit Feedback & Complete Project"
🏆  Handover Certificate — Documents tab
📋  Warranty terms in your signed contract

Common Questions
Can I make changes after approving the quotation?
Yes, but changes after approval are treated as separate add-on invoices and may affect production timelines. Raise changes as early as possible.
What is included in the project price?
All materials, sourcing, production, quality control, international shipping, customs clearance, and port charges. Installation is a separate add-on.
What if I'm not satisfied with the 3D rendering?
Request as many revisions as needed before approving. You are never obligated to approve a rendering that does not match your vision.
What if goods are damaged during shipping?
Westline manages all logistics including insurance. If goods arrive damaged, we handle the claim and replacement at no extra cost to you.
When exactly is the 10% final balance due?
When goods arrive in Ghana — at the same time as the 30% balance. Both invoices appear in your Financials tab simultaneously.
How do I get support during my project?
Use the Messages tab in your portal for a tracked record. Your PM responds within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, WhatsApp directly.
